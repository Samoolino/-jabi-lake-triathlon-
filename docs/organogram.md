```mermaid
    T --> S[Environmental Sensing]

    T --> R[Reporting]

    G --> G1[ESG]
    G --> G2[Compliance]

    E --> E1[Race Planning]
    E --> E2[Venue Setup]

    S --> S1[Air]
    S --> S2[Moisture]

    R --> R1[Live Reports]
    R --> R2[Alerts]

    P --> P1[Workforce]
    P --> P2[Performance]

    D --> D1[Data]
    D --> D2[Dashboard]
```
